About Weather You Run

Running isn't something that most people enjoy. I wanted to make an app that would make a simple aspect, such as figuring out what to wear so you're not freezing or overheating, just that much easier.

In WYR you can check the weather and see suggested running articles of clothing to help make your run more enjoyable. Should you want to track a run just create an account and log in. Feel free to make a short note about how your run went. What worked and what didn't?

The Creator

My name is Paloma. I am a web developer based in Seattle, WA. I built this app using, angular, express, mongoose and bootstrap. For fun I like to run, which inspired me to make this app!
